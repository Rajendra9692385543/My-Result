🎓 MyResult

Student Result & Credit Tracker Portal

🚀 Features

  • Student Result Viewer – Enter registration number & semester to view detailed results with CGPA chart.
  • Credit Tracker – Semester-wise cleared, backlog, pending credits with charts & progress bar.
  • Admin Panel – Secure login, bulk Excel uploads, upload history tracking.

🛠️ Tech Stack

Layer Tech
FrontendHTML, Bootstrap 5
BackendFlask (Python)
DatabaseSupabase (PostgreSQL)
ChartsChart.js
File UploadExcel (.xlsx via Pandas)

📈 Credit Calculation Logic

Total credits = sum of all course credits. Cleared credits are those with grades other than F or S. Backlogs are courses with grade F or S. Pending credits = required – (cleared + backlog). Visualized using pie charts and progress bars.
